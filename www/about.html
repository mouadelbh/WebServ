About 1337

The Future of IT Training. Today.

Our Philosophy: Code, Learn, Evolve

1337 is not just a school; it's a revolution in IT education. Inspired by the innovative 42 Network, 1337 offers a unique, tuition-free learning experience that is entirely project-based and peer-to-peer. We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their learning journey, fostering creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration.

There are no traditional teachers, no lectures, and no fixed schedules. Instead, students (often called "cadets") learn by doing, working on a series of challenging projects that cover a wide spectrum of IT skills, from basic programming to complex systems architecture, AI, cybersecurity, and more.

The "Piscine" - Dive In

The journey at 1337 begins with the "Piscine" (French for 'swimming pool'). This is an intensive, month-long coding bootcamp designed to test motivation, adaptability, and problem-solving skills. It's a challenging but rewarding experience that serves as the selection process and an introduction to our learning methodology.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Collaboration is at the heart of 1337. Students learn from and with each other, evaluating projects, sharing knowledge, and tackling challenges together. This system builds strong communication and teamwork skills, crucial in the tech industry.

Curriculum & Growth

The curriculum is structured like a video game, with levels to unlock and skills to master. Students progress at their own pace, choosing projects that align with their interests and career goals. This gamified approach makes learning engaging and motivating.

  • Core Curriculum: Building a strong foundation in C programming, Unix, algorithms, and data structures.
  • Specializations: Opportunities to delve into advanced fields such as web development, mobile app development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data science, and more.
  • Real-world Experience: Internships and industry partnerships provide practical experience and career opportunities.

Open to All, Free for All

1337 is open to anyone over 18, regardless of previous academic qualifications. The selection process focuses purely on talent, motivation, and logical reasoning. And importantly, the training is entirely free of charge, making high-quality tech education accessible to all.
